# AGENT DNA + GENERATIONAL EVOLUTION ENGINE

**Owner:** Genealogist (lineage and provenance authority)

The Genealogist records DNA proposals, parentage, mutations, inheritance, and
the Living Family Tree. Fish species may provide supporting assessments.

The Genealogist does not authorize activation or external action. Hybridization
and major-version proposals require the applicable policy checks and Quorum
decision. A lineage record becomes verified only when its content hash and
receipt references can be reproduced.
